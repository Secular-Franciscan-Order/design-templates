Is this for me?

Questions, gently answered

Who are the Franciscans?+

The spiritual family of St. Francis of Assisi (1181–1226) — friars, sisters, and lay men and women living his Gospel way of life.

Do you live like monks and nuns?+

No. We take no monastic vows. We keep our jobs, marry, raise families, and pray within everyday life.

Can anyone join?+

Any practicing Catholic, single or married, age 18 or older, who feels drawn to the Franciscan way.

What's expected of me?+

Daily prayer, regular Mass, faithful presence at our monthly gatherings, and a heart turned toward the poor and toward peace.
