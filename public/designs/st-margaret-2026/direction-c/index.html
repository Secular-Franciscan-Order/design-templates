Who We Are

We are lay Catholics - single and married, of every age and walk of life - living the Gospel in the footsteps of Saint Francis and Saint Clare. We keep our homes, our families, and our work, and within them we seek to make Christ's love visible: serving those who have less, befriending the lonely, and carrying hope into the city of Las Vegas.

Continue reading our profession +

We are not monks or nuns, and we take no monastic vows. We are the Secular Franciscan Order - the lay branch of the family Francis founded - promising to follow the Gospel in his spirit of poverty, humility, and joy. We gather in fraternity, we pray, and we go out again into ordinary life, that life itself might become Gospel.

Start by doing what is necessary, then what is possible, and suddenly you are doing the impossible.

The Path of Discernment

A journey, not a decision

Nothing is asked of you at the threshold. Joining unfolds slowly, over years - time enough to pray, to know us, and to listen for the Lord's voice.

i.
Orientation

Come, observe, and ask anything. A relaxed welcome that asks nothing of you in return.

~ 3 months
ii.
Inquiry

Explore the life of Francis and the Gospel alongside us, in unhurried fellowship.

~ 6-9 months
iii.
Candidacy

Deeper formation as you prepare your heart for profession.

~ 2 years
iv.
Profession

A lifelong promise to live the Gospel in the way of Francis.

for life

Is this for me?

About the Franciscans
Who are the Franciscans?+

We are the spiritual family of Saint Francis of Assisi, who left wealth behind to follow the poor and humble Christ. His family today numbers friars, sisters, and lay men and women - Secular Franciscans - who carry his Gospel way of life into the world.

What does "Secular Franciscan" mean?+

"Secular" means we live in the world - in our homes, families, and work - rather than in a monastery. We are fully Franciscan, and fully part of ordinary life.

Do you live like monks and nuns?+

No. Secular Franciscans are lay Catholics. We keep our homes, work, families, and responsibilities while following Jesus in the spirit of Saint Francis.

About joining
Can anyone join the Secular Franciscan Order?+

Any practicing Catholic in good standing - single or married, age 18 or older - who feels drawn to the Franciscan way is welcome to begin discerning with us.

How long does it take to join?+

The path is intentionally slow: orientation, inquiry, candidacy, and then profession. Each step gives you time to pray, learn, and discern freely.

What if I'm just curious and not ready to commit?+

Come as you are. Visiting a gathering is a simple way to listen, ask questions, and meet people without making a commitment.

What's expected of me
What is expected of me if I join?+

A life of daily prayer, regular participation in the Mass, faithful presence at our monthly gatherings, and a heart turned toward the poor and toward peace.

Do I have to wear a habit?+

No. Secular Franciscans generally wear the Tau cross, a simple sign associated with Saint Francis, rather than a religious habit.
