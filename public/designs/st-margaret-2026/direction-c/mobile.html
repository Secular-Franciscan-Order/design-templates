Who We Are

We are lay Catholics living the Gospel in the footsteps of Francis and Clare — keeping our homes, families, and work, and within them serving those who have less and carrying hope into Las Vegas.

Continue reading our profession +

We are not monks or nuns and take no vows. We are the lay branch of the family Francis founded, promising to follow the Gospel in his spirit of poverty, humility, and joy.

Frequently Asked

Is this for me?

Who are the Franciscans?+

The spiritual family of St. Francis of Assisi (1181–1226) — friars, sisters, and lay men and women carrying his Gospel way of life into the world.

Do you live like monks and nuns?+

No. We take no vows and live in our own homes, keeping our jobs and families.

Can anyone join?+

Any practicing Catholic, single or married, age 18 or older, drawn to the Franciscan way.

What's expected of me?+

Daily prayer, regular Mass, monthly gatherings, and a heart turned toward the poor and toward peace.
