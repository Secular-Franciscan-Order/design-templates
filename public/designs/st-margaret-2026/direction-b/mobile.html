Good questions

Is this for me?

Who are the Franciscans?+

Followers of St. Francis of Assisi (1181–1226) — friars, sisters, and lay people living his Gospel spirit in the everyday world.

Do you live like monks and nuns?+

Not at all — no vows, no monastery. We keep our jobs, marry, and raise families.

Can anyone join?+

Any practicing Catholic, single or married, age 18 or up, who feels drawn to this way of life.

What's expected of me?+

Daily prayer, regular Mass, monthly gatherings, and a heart open to the poor and to peace — lived in your own circumstances.
